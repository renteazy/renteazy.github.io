The smarter way to handle rent

Why we’re around
We’re on a mission to make renting a home more affordable and accessible for renters at no extra risk to property managers or landlords. It’s a win-win.

JOIN

The problem for renters

Renting a home can be difficult. Renters often face high move-in costs when asked to pay a first month's rent and a security deposit on a potential home. 76% of South African live paycheque to paycheque, indicating that a large proportion of potential renters are excluded from the market.

We believe that renting a home shouldn’t be financially straining and we’re working hard to solve it by removing the need for an upfront security deposit.

  • Security deposits are prohibitive
  • Security deposits alternatives make renting more accessible and inclusive

The problem for property manager

Renting a property is a two-way street. Property managers and landlords need security deposit protection for peace of mind. To help renters, we also need to help their property managers reduce or maintain risk. Property managers want to create the best resident experience possible while ensuring the business objectives and security concerns are met.

That’s why we plan to design products with property managers and not for property managers.

  • Zero risk
  • Reduce barriers of entry for renters

Security Deposit Alternative

Increase occupancy rates and velocity with a dynamic new tool for leasing. Introducing an alternative solution that replaces traditional cash security deposits with low-cost surety bonds. This product dramatically lowers move-in costs for your applicants, helping property managers increase lease conversions.

  • Zero risk
  • Increase occupancy

How it works

  • Potential renters pay a once-off fee for a low-cost surety bond.
  • At lease end, property managers present RentEasy with a statement of outstanding balances on a property.
  • We reimburse you within 4 days and recover outstanding balances from renters.

Security Deposit Alternative

Security deposit alternatives replace the need to pay high cash deposits when renting a property. This can be paid as either a one-time, non-refundable premium or a low cost, non-refundable monthly premium.
Instead of paying a high cash security deposit, RentEasy deposit gives renters the option to pay a fraction of the amount, which serves as a guarantee to the property owner or manager that they will be protected in the event that a renter fails to pay rent or damages the property.

  • Free up cash
  • Trusted 3rd party

How it works

  • Potential renters pay a once-off fee for a low-cost surety bond.
  • At lease end, if the renter is liable for damages or missed rent, property managers present RentEasy with a statement of outstanding balances on a property.
  • RentEasy reimburse the property manager within 4 days and recover outstanding balances from the renter.
  • Note: Renters are still liable for amounts owed to the property manager if rent is missed or the property is damaged.

Benefits for Renters

    Smarter Spending
  • Don’t take out a high-interest loan to pay a security deposit. Payless to move into a new home and use the money for savings or move-in expenses.
    • Remove hassle
  • Remove the hassle of sourcing and paying a security deposit when moving into a new home. Eliminate the headache of trying to get a security deposit back from landlords with more power than you and avoid lengthy legal battles.
    • Keep your cash
  • Choose hope you spend your money today, not a year from now. Use your security deposit to set up a savings account and protect your future.
